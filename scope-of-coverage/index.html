If you’ve purchased a Pro or Enterprise subscription from OpDemand, we…

Support

  • Provisioning hosts used as part of a Deis cluster
  • Installation of Deis platform components and utilities
  • Deploying and managing Twelve Factor applications on a Deis cluster
  • Connecting applications to backing services like databases, caches and queues
  • Integrating platform routers with front-end load balancers
  • Aggregation of log data for the platform and hosted applications
  • Platform administration including upgrades, backups and restores

Do Not Support

  • Issues related to cloud providers or underlying infrastructure
  • Problems with backing services like databases, caches and queues
  • Deploying or managing applications that do not follow Twelve Factor methodology
  • Using Deis on unsupported platforms or with unsupported 3rd-party software
  • Problems with 3rd-party software or services that are not related to Deis
  • Customization of Deis platform components
